Welcome to Mailly Manufacturing!

Mailly Manufacturing specializes in precision stainless steel shoulder screws, captive screws, dowel pins and shafting, produced with equal precision in standard and metric measurements. We follow customers' blueprints exactly, maintaining either Mil-Spec. 450208 or our customers' choice of any of the generally accepted industry performance specifications.

Company History

Founded in 1947 by Emil Mailly

Over the last 45 years the Mailly family name has become synonymous with service. Into the third generation, keeping our good name is as vital to us as ever. We protect it by careful, individual attention to each customer's needs and specifications.

We're equally at home with long and/or short runs and we provide guaranteed quick turn-around for both.

Our satisfied clients range from strictly commercial companies through general industrial to high-tech aerospace firms. Your company can rely on Mailly as well.

Products

Mailly applies state-of-the-art technology to fabricate precision machined parts to exacting specifications.

Our Swiss screw machines perform to tolerances of tenths of thousandths of an inch on parts up to 1" diameter, any length, any material.

And our technicians perform as if our reputation depends upon each piece... because it does.

Superior quality control is the prime objective of Mailly Manufacturing. Our raw materials specifications are demanding, following S.P.C. guidelines. And we're no less rigorous in every step of production and inspection.

That's why Mailly Manufacturing guarantees that any item we manufacture will meet your performance requirements!
